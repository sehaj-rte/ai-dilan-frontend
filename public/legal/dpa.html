1. Scope of this Agreement

This Agreement governs all Personal Data processed through the AI Persona Framework platform. It includes:

  • Data uploaded by the Client (text, images, audio, video)
  • AI-generated content (text responses, synthetic voice outputs)
  • Data processed by third-party sub-processors such as OpenAI and ElevenLabs
  • Metadata and logs generated during platform usage

The Company acts as Processor except where it processes Client account administration data, where it acts as an independent Controller.

2. Roles of the Parties

The Processor processes Protected Data solely on documented instructions from the Client. The Client is responsible for ensuring all uploaded data is lawful and that required consents are obtained.

  • The Client is solely responsible for obtaining consent from third parties appearing in uploaded content (voice, images, likeness).
  • The Processor shall not use uploaded data for model training or platform improvement without explicit written consent.
  • Each party is independently responsible for its Controller obligations where applicable.

3. Customer Responsibilities

The Client must comply with all Applicable Privacy Laws and ensure:

  • All processing instructions are lawful
  • All consents for third-party data (including biometric data) are properly obtained
  • Data subjects are informed of processing under Articles 13 and 14 UK GDPR
  • Special category data is processed only with Article 9 compliance
  • Requests from individuals are handled appropriately

4. Company Responsibilities

The Processor shall:

  • Process Personal Data only per Client instructions
  • Ensure staff confidentiality
  • Implement Article 32 GDPR security measures
  • Assist with Data Subject rights
  • Assist with DPIAs, breach notifications, and Article 36 obligations

5. Processing Instructions

The Processor will only process Protected Data per written Client instructions. If the Processor believes an instruction may breach privacy laws, it will notify the Client.

Protected Data will not be used for AI model training or service improvement unless explicit consent is granted.

Transfers outside the UK/EEA will rely on:

The Processor shall provide logs, audit reports, and evidence of compliance. One audit per year is permitted unless triggered by a breach.
