Expert Platform Privacy Policy

Expert-Specific Privacy Notice: This privacy policy applies specifically to data collected and processed through our AI Expert consultation platform.

1. Expert Consultation Data Collection

When you interact with our AI experts, we collect specific types of information to provide optimal consultation services:

Data Type Purpose Retention Period
Consultation queries Service delivery & improvement 90 days
Expert interaction history Personalization & context 1 year
Session metadata Analytics & optimization 2 years
Feedback & ratings Quality assurance Indefinite (anonymized)

2. Expert Knowledge Base Privacy

Our expert systems are trained on diverse knowledge sources while maintaining strict privacy standards:

  • No Personal Data Training: Expert models are not trained on individual user conversations
  • Anonymized Learning: Only aggregated, anonymized patterns improve expert capabilities
  • Secure Processing: All expert consultations use end-to-end encryption
  • Isolated Sessions: Each consultation is processed independently

3. Sensitive Information Handling

For expert consultations involving sensitive data:

3.1 Professional Confidentiality

We implement enhanced security measures for consultations in regulated domains (legal, medical, financial):

  • Enhanced encryption protocols
  • Restricted data access
  • Automatic redaction of PII
  • Compliance with industry-specific regulations

3.2 Data Minimization

We only collect data essential for expert consultation delivery and automatically purge unnecessary information.

4. Expert Session Recording

Expert consultation sessions may be recorded for:

  • Quality assurance and training purposes
  • Dispute resolution
  • Service improvement
  • Compliance verification

Users can request session deletion within 30 days of consultation.

5. Third-Party Expert Integrations

Some expert services may integrate with third-party tools and databases. We ensure:

  • All integrations meet our privacy standards
  • Data sharing is explicitly disclosed
  • Users can opt-out of specific integrations
  • Third parties are contractually bound to privacy terms

6. Your Expert Data Rights

As an expert platform user, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your consultation history
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Delete your expert interaction data
  • Portability: Export your consultation records
  • Restriction: Limit processing of your data
  • Objection: Opt-out of certain data uses

7. Expert Platform Security

We implement industry-leading security measures:

  • 256-bit AES encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • SOC 2 Type II compliance
  • GDPR and CCPA compliant processes
