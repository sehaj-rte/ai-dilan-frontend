Expert Platform Data Processing Agreement

Expert DPA Notice: This Data Processing Agreement governs the processing of personal data through our AI Expert consultation platform in compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable data protection regulations.

1. Parties and Definitions

Role Entity Responsibilities
Data Controller Customer/User Determines purposes and means of processing personal data during expert consultations
Data Processor YOORZ.AI LIMITED Processes personal data on behalf of the controller through expert platform services
Sub-processors Authorized third parties Limited processing under strict contractual obligations

2. Expert Platform Processing Activities

2.1 Categories of Personal Data

The expert platform may process the following categories of personal data:

  • Consultation Content: Questions, requests, and information shared with AI experts
  • Professional Data: Industry-specific information relevant to expert consultations
  • Technical Data: Session logs, interaction patterns, and system performance metrics
  • Account Data: User identification and subscription information

2.2 Categories of Data Subjects

  • Expert platform users and subscribers
  • Individuals mentioned in consultation content
  • Professional contacts referenced in expert sessions

3. Processing Purposes and Legal Basis

Processing Purpose Legal Basis Retention Period
Expert consultation delivery Contract performance Duration of consultation + 90 days
Service improvement Legitimate interest Anonymized - indefinite
Quality assurance Legitimate interest 1 year
Compliance monitoring Legal obligation As required by law

4. Expert Platform Security Measures

4.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Access Controls: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
  • Network Security: Firewalls, intrusion detection, and DDoS protection
  • Data Isolation: Logical separation of expert consultation data

4.2 Organizational Measures

  • Regular security training for all personnel
  • Background checks for employees with data access
  • Incident response procedures and breach notification protocols
  • Regular security audits and compliance assessments

5. Sub-processing for Expert Services

YOORZ.AI LIMITED may engage sub-processors for expert platform operations, including:

  • Cloud Infrastructure: Secure hosting and computing resources
  • AI Model Providers: Specialized expert knowledge systems
  • Analytics Services: Performance monitoring and optimization
  • Security Services: Threat detection and prevention

All sub-processors are bound by equivalent data protection obligations.

6. Data Subject Rights in Expert Context

We facilitate the exercise of data subject rights for expert platform users:

  • Right of Access: Consultation history and processed data
  • Right to Rectification: Correction of inaccurate consultation records
  • Right to Erasure: Deletion of expert interaction data
  • Right to Data Portability: Export of consultation records in structured format
  • Right to Restriction: Limitation of processing activities
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of certain processing purposes

7. International Data Transfers

Expert platform data may be transferred internationally with appropriate safeguards:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfers
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Binding Corporate Rules for intra-group transfers
  • Certification schemes and codes of conduct

8. Incident Management and Breach Notification

8.1 Incident Response

In case of a personal data breach affecting expert platform data:

  • Immediate containment and assessment within 1 hour
  • Customer notification within 24 hours
  • Regulatory notification within 72 hours (where required)
  • Detailed incident report within 7 days

9. Audit and Compliance

YOORZ.AI LIMITED maintains comprehensive audit capabilities:

  • Annual third-party security audits
  • SOC 2 Type II compliance reports
  • GDPR compliance assessments
  • Customer audit rights upon reasonable notice
